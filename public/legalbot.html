Legal Self-Help Chatbot

Myself, and three others were a team of interns tasked with re-designing the Superior Court of California, County of Contra Costa's Self-Help Legal Website. Over the course of six months we researched the Small Claims process, along with different AI and Machine Learning Technologies. I worked with a skilled backend developer, and together we made the AI Chatbot shown in the demo below. I learned React specifically for this projcet, and designed and implemented the UI / UX for the chatbot.
